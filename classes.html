make.br is currently putting together a list of classes that will be offered. If you are interested in teaching a class or reccomending a class that we should be taught, then send us an email to let us know!

Do you have a skill? Do you think other people would be interested in that skill? We think they would! Come teach that skill in a class at make.br!

Currently we are looking for teachers with the following skillsets:

  • Graphic Design(Photoshop, Ilustrator/Sketch)
  • Electronics and Microcontrollers (Arduinos, Raspberry Pi's, Chip, etc.)
  • Jewelry Making
  • Programming Languages (Python, C#, Swift, HTML/CSS, Javascript, etc.)
  • And anything else you can imagine!

We do not currently have a space that is equipped for woodworking, metalworking or welding. If you have a space or might know of space where we could have classes on these subjects let us know!

Email Lance@makebr.com with any questions or suggestions
